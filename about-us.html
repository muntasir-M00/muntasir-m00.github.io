About TechSergeant

TechSergeant is a student project website designed to provide practical tech solutions for laptops and desktops. It focuses on resolving hardware and software issues—primarily software-related—with detailed video guidance and step-by-step written processes. Additionally, the site offers comprehensive guides for installing various operating systems, including Windows, Linux, Chrome OS, Tiny10/11, and Windows 10 LTSC.
